About Us

Viber India is a group with Indo Russian Business origin which has proved itself as a professional, customer centric and reliable company for all your travel and ground transportation needs.Viber India was founded in year 2004 aiming the Common Wealth Games. In 2013, Soyuz Tours & Travels and Viber Car Rentals were formally merged under the umbrella of Viber Mobility & Hospitality Private Limited for better market focus and concentration. The aim was to provide all travel solutions under one roof. Soyuz Tours & Travels was established more than 10 years ago and is now one of the most renowned travel companies amongst the working expats and tourist visiting India for business / leisure tourism purpose. Viber Mobility & Hospitality now has fully operational offices / franchisee in almost 45 + Indian Cities and 4 International Locations which Includes Russia, Kathmandu, Hongkong & Bhutan. At Viber India, we concentrate on staying ahead by providing well maintained cars with best of chauffeurs chosen across the industry. We believe in constantly challenging the way we operate and by looking to continuously improve at all levels of business. Our Services: At Viber Mobility & Hospitality Private Limited, we believe in providing one stop solution to all your travel needs. We offer diversified range of travel products that includes: 1. PAN India Car rental Services. 2. International Car Rental Services. 3. Domestic & International Hotel Reservations. 4. Domestic & International Tour Packages. 5. Concierge Services. 6. Agent Representation. 7. Customized Tours for Group Travelers and Individual Tourist. 8. Medical Tourism & Air Ambulance Services. 9. Air Charter Services. 10. Forex Services & Exchange. 11. Meeting, Incentives, Convention & Events (MICE). “RELIABLE & CUSTOMIZED TRAVEL SERVICES” Mission & Vision Statement: Viber India first came over the web in the year 2005 and it actually started as an inbound tour operator. Through Years of hard work and references across from satisfied clients; Viber India reached to position of being market leader through focusing on core values such as strong customer service and excellent value for money. Mission: To be Travel Industry’s Leader and set bench marks in travel domain. Vision: To be the world’s most prestigious and renowned hospitality brand.
